Casatiello Recipe


Description:

Casatiello is a savory Neapolitan Easter bread, enriched with lard, pecorino and cured meats, with whole eggs set into the top.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Lard
  • Pecorino and Parmesan
  • Salami and cured meats
  • Whole eggs
  • Black pepper

Instructions:

  1. Knead the dough with flour, lard and plenty of pepper.
  2. Let it rise, then roll it out and fill with cheese and meats.
  3. Roll into a ring and set the whole eggs on top.
  4. Let it rise again, then bake at 180°C for about 50 minutes.
