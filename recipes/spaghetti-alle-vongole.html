Spaghetti alle vongole Recipe


Description:

A classic of Neapolitan cuisine: spaghetti tossed with fresh clams, garlic, olive oil and parsley.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Spaghetti
  • Fresh clams
  • Garlic
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Parsley
  • Chili pepper (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Purge the clams in salted water for a couple of hours.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil, then add the clams until they open.
  3. Cook the spaghetti al dente and toss it with the clams.
  4. Finish with chopped parsley and serve.
