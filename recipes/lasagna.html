Neapolitan Lasagna


Description:

Neapolitan lasagna (lasagna di Carnevale) is a rich baked pasta made with Neapolitan ragù, ricotta, tiny fried meatballs, fior di latte and hard-boiled eggs. No béchamel here — this is the southern version.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Neapolitan ragù (slow-cooked meat sauce)
  • Ricotta
  • Small fried meatballs (polpettine)
  • Fior di latte or mozzarella
  • Hard-boiled eggs
  • Grated Parmesan or pecorino

Instructions:

  1. Slow-cook the Neapolitan ragù for several hours.
  2. Fry the tiny meatballs and boil the eggs.
  3. Loosen the ricotta with a little ragù sauce.
  4. Layer pasta with ragù, ricotta, meatballs, fior di latte, sliced eggs and grated cheese.
  5. Repeat the layers, then bake at 180°C for about 40 minutes.
