Lasagna Recipe


Description:

Lasagna is a baked pasta dish made of layers of pasta, meat sauce and béchamel. A classic of Italian cuisine.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Meat sauce (ragù)
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Grated Parmesan
  • Mozzarella

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook pasta sheets until al dente.
  3. Layer meat sauce, pasta, béchamel, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  4. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes until bubbly and golden.
